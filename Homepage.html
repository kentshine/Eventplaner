About Us

Modern Arts College, Kochi is committed to provide all requirements in curricular and co-curricular areas of Arts Education to our students and mould them with knowledge, Soft skills, Physical Education and Ethics to enable them become Professionals of International Standards. The College is also committed to strive for continual improvement of its Quality Management System through customer satisfaction in terms of achieving Academic Excellence, Total Personality Development and excellent placement opportunities for the students

Our Silver Jubliee

Modern Arts College, Kochi is celebrating its silver jubilee this year. In connection with the Anniversery celebration, the College has planned various events which will benefit the student community and also the society around.
